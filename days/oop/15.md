# Day 15: Inheritance & Polymorphism - Practice

## 📝 Today's Tasks

1.  **Design Challenge:** Model payments, notifications, or shapes using a contract + 2–3 implementations.
2.  **Polymorphic Loop:** Process mixed implementations through one abstraction.
3.  **Composition Pass:** Share at least one behavior via composition.
4.  **Critique:** Note one place you avoided deep inheritance on purpose.

## 📖 Task Explanations

Consolidate inheritance, interfaces, polymorphism, and composition into one small design. Favor shallow structures and clear contracts.

## 💡 What You'll Gain

A polymorphic design with interfaces/contracts, multiple implementations, and intentional composition.

## 📚 Dictionary for Developers

*   **Polymorphic Collection:** A collection of mixed subtypes used via a shared type.
*   **Design Critique:** A short review of why the structure is sound.
*   **Shallow Hierarchy:** An inheritance tree kept intentionally small.
