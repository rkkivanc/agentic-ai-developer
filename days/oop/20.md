# Day 20: SOLID, Patterns & Practice - Capstone Model & Review

## 📝 Today's Tasks

1.  **Capstone Domain:** Build a cohesive OOP model (shop orders, course enrollments, or task board).
2.  **Apply Principles:** Show encapsulation, at least one abstraction contract, and one SOLID win.
3.  **Optional Pattern:** Use Factory or Strategy only if it clearly helps.
4.  **Reflection:** Write what you learned across 20 days and what you will practice next.

## 📖 Task Explanations

Your capstone should be small but professional: clear objects, protected invariants, programming to interfaces where useful, and SOLID-minded structure. Reflection locks the learning so OOP becomes a habit—not just vocabulary.

## 💡 What You'll Gain

By the end of today, you will have a finished OOP mini-project demonstrating fundamentals through SOLID thinking, plus a clear personal review of your progress.

## 📚 Dictionary for Developers

*   **Capstone:** A culminating project that integrates skills from the whole track.
*   **Cohesive Model:** Objects that fit together with clear boundaries.
*   **Professional OOP:** Using objects, encapsulation, abstraction, and SOLID with judgment.
*   **Reflective Practice:** Reviewing what you learned to reinforce lasting skill.
