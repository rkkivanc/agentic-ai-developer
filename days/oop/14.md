# Day 14: Inheritance & Polymorphism - Composition over Inheritance

## 📝 Today's Tasks

1.  **Spot Misuse:** Take a hierarchy that feels forced and list why inheritance is awkward.
2.  **Compose Instead:** Replace inheritance with a field that delegates to a helper object.
3.  **Behavior Sharing:** Share behavior via composed strategy/helper rather than subclassing.
4.  **Rule of Thumb:** Write when you prefer composition vs inheritance.

## 📖 Task Explanations

Composition builds complex behavior by combining objects. Prefer composition when you mainly want reuse, not a true 'is-a' relationship. Many design problems melt away when hierarchies shrink.

## 💡 What You'll Gain

You will refactor inheritance into composition and articulate a clear preference rule.

## 📚 Dictionary for Developers

*   **Composition:** Building behavior by combining objects that collaborate.
*   **Delegation:** Forwarding work to another object.
*   **Has-A Relationship:** An object containing another object.
*   **Is-A Relationship:** A true subtype relationship suitable for inheritance.
