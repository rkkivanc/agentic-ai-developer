# Day 8: Encapsulation & Abstraction - Abstraction and Interfaces of Thought

## 📝 Today's Tasks

1.  **Raise the Level:** Replace a low-level sequence of steps with a named method that expresses intent.
2.  **Stable Abstraction:** Design a small interface/protocol (even on paper) that multiple classes could satisfy.
3.  **Caller Simplicity:** Refactor a caller so it depends on the abstraction, not concrete steps.
4.  **Document Intent:** Name methods after outcomes (`checkout`, `schedule`) not machinery.

## 📖 Task Explanations

Abstraction means showing the essential idea and hiding messy details. Good names are abstraction tools. When callers depend on intent-level methods, your design stays flexible as internals change.

## 💡 What You'll Gain

You will create intent-revealing abstractions and reduce caller coupling to implementation details.

## 📚 Dictionary for Developers

*   **Abstraction:** Focusing on essential behavior while hiding details.
*   **Interface (concept):** A contract of operations without committing to one implementation.
*   **Coupling:** How strongly one module depends on another.
*   **Intent-Revealing Name:** A name that describes purpose, not implementation.
