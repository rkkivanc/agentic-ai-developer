### 🚀 OOP Learning Roadmap: Day 1 to Day 20 (Foundational Skills to Practical Competence)

| Day | Focus Area | Content Details | Goal (From Foundation to Competence) |
| :---: | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1-5** | **OOP Fundamentals** | Objects vs procedures, classes, instances, attributes, methods, constructors, and a first mini-model. | Think in objects and create simple class-based models. |
| **6-10** | **Encapsulation & Abstraction** | Access control, getters/setters, invariants, abstract types, and hiding complexity behind clear APIs. | Protect state and expose only what callers need. |
| **11-15** | **Inheritance & Polymorphism** | Inheritance, overriding, interfaces/abstract contracts, polymorphism, and composition vs inheritance. | Reuse behavior safely and program to abstractions. |
| **16-20** | **SOLID, Patterns & Practice** | SOLID principles, common patterns (Factory, Strategy, Observer), refactoring legacy code, and a capstone model. | Apply OOP professionally in real designs. |
