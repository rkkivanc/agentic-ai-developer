# Day 75: Observability & Resilience - Practice

## 📝 Today's Tasks

1.  **Instrument MVP:** Add structured logs, `/metrics`, and basic tracing to your service.
2.  **Simulate Failure:** Force database errors and observe logs, metrics, and breaker behavior.
3.  **Dashboard Sketch:** Draw panels you would monitor: RPS, error rate, latency, saturation.
4.  **Runbook Notes:** Write short steps for on-call when error rate spikes.

## 📖 Task Explanations

Today makes your MVP operable—not just functional. Simulate failures to see telemetry in action.

Sketch dashboards even if you lack Grafana yet—decide what matters before incidents happen.

Short runbooks turn panic into procedure.

## 💡 What You'll Gain

By the end of today, your service will emit logs, metrics, and traces with notes for operating it.

## 📚 Dictionary for Developers

*   **Runbook:** Documented steps for responding to operational incidents.
*   **Error Rate:** Percentage of requests failing, a key service health signal.
*   **Saturation:** How fully utilized resources like CPU, memory, or DB pools are.
*   **Failure Injection:** Deliberately causing errors to validate resilience and observability.
*   **Operable Service:** Software you can understand and debug in production, not a black box.
