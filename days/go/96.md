# Day 96: Capstone & Professional Delivery - Capstone Planning

## 📝 Today's Tasks

1.  **Choose Capstone Scope:** Pick an MVP extension or new service combining prior skills.
2.  **Write Requirements:** List endpoints, SLAs, auth, data, and async needs.
3.  **Draw Architecture:** Sketch components, databases, queues, and observability.
4.  **Create Milestone Plan:** Break work into daily deliverables through Day 100.

## 📖 Task Explanations

The capstone is your proof of professional readiness. Choose scope ambitious but finishable in five days—depth beats sprawling half-features.

Requirements and architecture diagrams align you before coding furiously. Milestones keep you honest about progress.

This is your story to tell interviewers and teammates.

## 💡 What You'll Gain

By the end of today, you will have a scoped capstone plan with architecture and milestones.

## 📚 Dictionary for Developers

*   **Capstone:** A culminating project integrating skills learned across the curriculum.
*   **SLA (Service Level Agreement):** Targets for availability, latency, or support response.
*   **Milestone:** A checkpoint delivering a coherent subset of functionality.
*   **Scope Control:** Keeping project boundaries realistic to finish with quality.
*   **Architecture Sketch:** A high-level diagram of components and their interactions.
