## 📚 Dictionary for Developers

*   **Authentication:** Verifying who the caller is, often via tokens or sessions.
*   **Authorization:** Determining whether the caller may access a specific field or mutation.
*   **JWT (JSON Web Token):** A compact, signed token often placed in the Authorization header.
*   **RBAC (Role-Based Access Control):** Permissions assigned by roles rather than individual users.
*   **Field Guard:** Logic that allows or denies access to a single field based on context.
