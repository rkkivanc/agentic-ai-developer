## 📚 Dictionary for Developers

*   **Schema Governance:** Processes and rules for evolving a shared GraphQL schema safely.
*   **Deprecation:** Marking fields or types as scheduled for removal with `@deprecated`.
*   **Breaking Change:** A schema change that can cause existing client operations to fail.
*   **Naming Convention:** Consistent rules for type, field, and argument names across the schema.
*   **Domain Boundary:** A logical separation of types and operations by business area.
