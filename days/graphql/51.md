## 📖 Task Explanations

Subscriptions fit when data changes unpredictably and near-real-time matters. They are not free—persistent connections consume server and proxy resources.

Start from product need: chat and live dashboards justify subscriptions; nightly batch reports do not.

## 💡 What You'll Gain

By the end of today, you will identify appropriate subscription use cases and sketch subscription operations in your schema.

## 📚 Dictionary for Developers

*   **Subscription Operation:** Long-lived GraphQL operation streaming server events.
*   **Polling:** Repeated queries on an interval to approximate live data.
*   **Push Model:** Server-initiated updates sent to subscribed clients.
*   **Realtime Feature:** Product capability requiring sub-second update delivery.
*   **Connection Cost:** Infrastructure overhead of maintaining open client sockets.
