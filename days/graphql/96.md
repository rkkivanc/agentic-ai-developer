# Day 96: Capstone & Professional Delivery - Capstone Planning and Architecture

## 📝 Today's Tasks

1.  **Choose a Capstone Domain:** Pick a project such as a task platform, marketplace, or content API.
2.  **Draft Schema and Architecture:** Sketch types, key mutations, auth model, and service boundaries.
3.  **Define Success Criteria:** List features proving schema design, auth, testing, caching, and deployment skills.
4.  **Create a Milestone Plan:** Break Days 97–99 into build, harden, and deploy phases.

## 📖 Task Explanations

Your capstone should demonstrate professional GraphQL engineering—not just a tutorial clone. Choose a domain you care about so you finish strong.

Plan auth, pagination, and at least one performance optimization upfront. Retrofitting these is harder than designing them in.

## 💡 What You'll Gain

By the end of today, you will have a clear capstone plan with schema outline, success criteria, and milestones.

## 📚 Dictionary for Developers

*   **Capstone Architecture:** High-level design for the final project combining curriculum skills.
*   **Production Readiness:** Checklist covering security, tests, monitoring, and deployment.
*   **Deployment Pipeline:** Automated steps building, testing, and releasing the GraphQL service.
*   **Observability Stack:** Combined logging, metrics, and tracing tools for production APIs.
*   **Technical Portfolio:** Collection of projects demonstrating professional engineering ability.
