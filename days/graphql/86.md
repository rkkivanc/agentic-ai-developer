## 📖 Task Explanations

Federation lets teams own independent GraphQL services that compose into one graph for clients. It solves organizational scaling, not just technical scaling.

Before adopting federation, ensure you need it. Small teams often benefit from a well-designed monolith longer than expected.

## 💡 What You'll Gain

## 📚 Dictionary for Developers

*   **Federation:** Architecture where independent subgraphs compose into one supergraph.
*   **Subgraph:** A service-owned GraphQL schema participating in a federated graph.
*   **Supergraph:** The unified graph exposed by a gateway composing multiple subgraphs.
*   **Schema Stitching:** Combining separate schemas programmatically, often for legacy services.
*   **Gateway:** A router that delegates fields to the appropriate subgraph or service.
