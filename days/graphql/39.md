## 📚 Dictionary for Developers

*   **Nullability:** Whether a field or input may be absent or explicitly null.
*   **Validation:** Checking inputs and business rules before executing mutations.
*   **Error Extension:** Extra structured data attached to a GraphQL error for clients or logging.
*   **User Error:** An expected failure such as invalid input, safe to show end users.
*   **System Error:** An unexpected failure such as database outage, logged but not exposed in detail.
