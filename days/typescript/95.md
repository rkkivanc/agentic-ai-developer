# Day 95: Team Practices & Migration - Practice and Review

## 📝 Today's Tasks

1.  **Review Simulation:** Perform a type-focused review on a provided or self-authored messy diff.
2.  **Migration Plan:** Draft a 4-week incremental migration plan for a hypothetical JS codebase.
3.  **Monorepo Checklist:** Audit your projects against monorepo, ADR, and documentation best practices.
4.  **Retrospective:** Note three team practices you will adopt on your next real project.

## 📖 Task Explanations

Practice and review day for team practices. The review simulation builds muscle memory—spot `any`, missing schemas, leaky exports quickly. The migration plan forces prioritization: which modules first, what metrics, what risks.

Auditing against checklists reveals gaps in your own projects—missing CONTRIBUTING, no ADRs, no affected CI. The retrospective personalizes lessons—team practices only matter if you actually adopt them.

You are preparing to work on teams, not just solo projects.

## 💡 What You'll Gain

By the end of today, you will have practiced type-focused review, drafted a migration plan, and committed to concrete team practices for future work.

## 📚 Dictionary for Developers

*   **Review Simulation:** Practicing code review on realistic diffs to build skills.
*   **Migration Plan:** A phased schedule for converting JavaScript to TypeScript.
*   **Best Practice Audit:** Checking a project against professional workflow standards.
*   **Practice Retrospective:** Personal notes on habits to carry into team environments.
*   **Professional Workflow:** Review, documentation, CI, and migration habits of mature teams.
