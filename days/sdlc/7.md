# Day 7: Requirements & Design - High-Level Design and Architecture Sketch

## 📝 Today's Tasks

1.  **Context Diagram:** Draw who uses the system and what external systems it talks to.
2.  **Component Sketch:** Box out major parts (UI, API, DB, jobs) and their responsibilities.
3.  **Data Sketch:** List main entities and relationships for your sample feature.
4.  **Trade-off Note:** Write one design decision and the alternative you rejected.

## 📖 Task Explanations

Design bridges requirements and code. You do not need UML perfection—you need shared understanding of boundaries, data, and trade-offs before large implementation effort.

## 💡 What You'll Gain

You will produce a simple architecture/context sketch and document at least one explicit design trade-off.

## 📚 Dictionary for Developers

*   **Architecture:** The high-level structure and responsibilities of a software system.
*   **Context Diagram:** A view showing the system and its external actors/systems.
*   **Component:** A major building block with a clear responsibility.
*   **Trade-off:** A choice that gains one quality at the cost of another.
