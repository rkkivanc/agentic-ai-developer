# Day 5: Requirements & Design - Gathering and Clarifying Needs

## 📝 Today's Tasks

1.  **Stakeholder Questions:** Write 10 discovery questions for a sample feature.
2.  **Problem vs Solution:** Separate user problems from premature technical solutions.
3.  **Constraints:** List business, time, legal, and technical constraints that shape requirements.
4.  **Ambiguity Hunt:** Take a vague request ('make it faster') and rewrite it as clarifying questions.

## 📖 Task Explanations

Requirements start with understanding problems, users, and constraints—not jumping to frameworks. Good discovery questions prevent building the wrong thing quickly.

## 💡 What You'll Gain

You will practice discovery questioning and turn vague requests into clearer problem statements.

## 📚 Dictionary for Developers

*   **Requirement:** A statement of needed capability, quality, or constraint.
*   **Discovery:** Early research to understand users, needs, and context.
*   **Constraint:** A limiting factor such as budget, deadline, compliance, or tech stack.
*   **Ambiguous Requirement:** A need stated so vaguely it can be interpreted many ways.
