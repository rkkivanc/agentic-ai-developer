# Day 4: SDLC Fundamentals - Practice

## 📝 Today's Tasks

1.  **Case Study:** Invent a small product idea and choose Waterfall, Agile, or Hybrid—justify in 5 bullets.
2.  **Phase Checklist:** Create a one-page checklist of artifacts you expect per phase for that product.
3.  **Risk Flag:** Identify the riskiest phase for your idea and how you'd mitigate it.
4.  **Teach-back:** Explain SDLC to a non-developer friend in under 2 minutes (write the script).

## 📖 Task Explanations

Practice turns vocabulary into judgment. Picking a model and naming artifacts forces clarity about what 'professional delivery' means for your context.

## 💡 What You'll Gain

A short case study with a chosen delivery model, phase artifacts, and a clear risk mitigation note.

## 📚 Dictionary for Developers

*   **Case Study:** A worked example used to practice decisions.
*   **Artifact:** A document, diagram, or product piece produced during development.
*   **Mitigation:** An action that reduces the impact or likelihood of a risk.
