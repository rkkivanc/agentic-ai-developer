# Day 8: Requirements & Design - Practice Project & Review

## 📝 Today's Tasks

1.  **Mini Spec:** Produce a one-pager: problem, users, 5 stories, acceptance criteria, constraints.
2.  **Design Attachments:** Add your context/component sketch to the one-pager.
3.  **Review Pass:** Have a peer (or yourself tomorrow) mark unclear parts and revise once.
4.  **Ready-to-Build Check:** Confirm a developer could start without guessing core intent.

## 📖 Task Explanations

This consolidates requirements and design into a lightweight spec—the kind of artifact healthy teams actually use. Clarity beats length.

## 💡 What You'll Gain

A concise, reviewable mini-spec that is ready to guide implementation.

## 📚 Dictionary for Developers

*   **Spec / Specification:** A document describing what to build and important constraints.
*   **One-pager:** A short summary artifact meant to be read quickly.
*   **Ready for Development:** Requirements/design clear enough to implement without constant guesswork.
