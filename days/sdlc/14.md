# Day 14: Operate, Improve & Capstone - Maintenance, Monitoring, and Incidents

## 📝 Today's Tasks

1.  **Maintenance Types:** Contrast corrective, adaptive, perfective, and preventive maintenance with examples.
2.  **Signals:** List metrics/logs you'd watch for a simple web API.
3.  **Incident Outline:** Write a mini incident timeline (detect → mitigate → fix → review).
4.  **On-call Empathy:** Note what information makes incidents easier to handle.

## 📖 Task Explanations

Most of a product's life is after launch. Maintenance and operations keep value alive. Monitoring and calm incident process are part of SDLC—not an afterthought.

## 💡 What You'll Gain

You will classify maintenance work and outline a basic monitoring + incident response approach.

## 📚 Dictionary for Developers

*   **Maintenance:** Ongoing work to keep software useful, safe, and current after release.
*   **Monitoring:** Watching health/performance signals of running systems.
*   **Incident:** An unplanned disruption or degradation of service.
*   **Post-Incident Review:** A blameless look at causes and improvements after an incident.
