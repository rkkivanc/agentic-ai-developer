# Day 13: Operate, Improve & Capstone - Deployment and Environments

## 📝 Today's Tasks

1.  **Environment Map:** Distinguish local, staging, and production responsibilities.
2.  **Promotion Path:** Describe how a change moves across environments.
3.  **Config Awareness:** List what must differ per environment (secrets, URLs, feature flags).
4.  **Unsafe Shortcut:** Explain why 'deploy untested hotfixes straight to prod' is risky—and a safer alternative.

## 📖 Task Explanations

Operations begin at deploy. Environments exist to reduce risk. Promoting through staging with proper config discipline is core SDLC hygiene.

## 💡 What You'll Gain

You will explain environments, promotion, and safer alternatives to reckless production hotfixes.

## 📚 Dictionary for Developers

*   **Environment:** A deployment target with its own config and purpose (dev/stage/prod).
*   **Staging:** A pre-production environment for realistic validation.
*   **Promotion:** Moving a build/artifact from one environment to the next.
*   **Hotfix:** An urgent fix; still benefits from minimal verification and auditability.
