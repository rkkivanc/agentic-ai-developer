# Day 16: Operate, Improve & Capstone - Full-Cycle Capstone Plan

## 📝 Today's Tasks

1.  **Pick a Feature:** Choose a small product feature to plan through the entire SDLC.
2.  **Full Packet:** Produce requirements (stories+AC), design sketch, build/test plan, release checklist, and ops notes.
3.  **Model Choice:** State Waterfall/Agile/Hybrid choice and why.
4.  **Reflection:** Write what phase you underestimate most and how you'll practice it next.

## 📖 Task Explanations

This capstone proves you can think in full life cycles—not only write code. A thin end-to-end plan is more valuable than a huge unfinished document. Reflection builds professional self-awareness.

## 💡 What You'll Gain

By the end of today, you will have a complete mini SDLC packet for one feature—from requirements to operations—and a clear personal improvement focus.

## 📚 Dictionary for Developers

*   **Capstone Plan:** A culminating exercise integrating all SDLC phases on one feature.
*   **End-to-End Thinking:** Considering the whole path from idea to operations.
*   **Professional Self-awareness:** Knowing which parts of delivery you need more practice in.
*   **Life Cycle Packet:** A bundled set of lightweight artifacts covering each phase.
